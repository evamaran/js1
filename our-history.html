Our History

We don't just make jackets. We make weather irrelevant.

RainyDays started with a simple idea: comfort shouldn't end where the trail begins. Whether you're hiking through sideways rain or navigating a Monday morning downpour, our gear is built to keep you dry, warm, and just a little smug about it.

We believe adventure lives in the everyday. That pushing the comfort zone doesn't always mean climbing a mountain — it might mean walking to the store when the sky looks angry. Our jackets are designed for those moments - where you choose movement over excuses, and style over soggy.

From fjord breezes to city gusts, we've tested our shells in the kind of weather that makes umbrellas cry. And we're just getting started.

RainyDays. Pushing the Comfort Zone — one storm at a time.
